          World Map

          An interactive map of every country — borders, capitals, flags and facts — grouped into continents, with overseas territories drawn in. The sidebar has two modes at the top: Explore (browse the map) and Quiz (test yourself).

          Explore

          • Countries tab — search the list; click a country to zoom in and select it.
          • Continents tab — click a continent to highlight its countries and see totals; expand it to list them.
          • Sort by name, population or area (the value is shown after the name).
          • Hover a country for a flag · name · capital tooltip at the cursor.
          • Select a country for a fact panel (population, area, GDP, currency, languages…) plus dashed lines to its territories (e.g. Alaska, Greenland, Réunion).
          • Show names / capitals / flags toggles, and Isolate to hide everything except the selection.
          • Names link to Wikipedia. Click the ocean to clear the selection.

          Quiz

          Switch to the Quiz tab (top of the sidebar) for several rounds with a running score:

          • By name — click the named country on the map. By flag / By capital — answer by Click on map or Select country (search by name).
          • Continent — a country is named; click any country in its continent on the map. The correct continent turns green, a wrong pick red.
          • Neighbour — find all countries that border the target. Choose how to answer: Click on map or Select countries (search by name), then Check. Found neighbours show green, missed ones and wrong picks are marked too.
          • Wrong answers are revealed on the map (correct green, wrong red) with flag and name; use Skip or Next → to move on.
